Common questions

How quickly do you respond to estimate requests?

During business hours (Mon–Fri 9am–6pm) we typically respond within 2–4 hours. If you send a request in the evening or over the weekend, you'll hear from us first thing the next working day. WhatsApp is always the fastest route.

Do you charge for estimates?

No — estimates are always completely free and there's no obligation to proceed. We don't charge a call-out fee either. We only charge once the work is agreed and completed.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of North Dublin including Malahide, Swords, Portmarnock, Sutton, Clontarf, Artane, Beaumont, Santry, Drumcondra, Coolock, Raheny and Finglas, as well as surrounding northside postcodes. Not sure if we cover your area? Just call us on 01 691 7151.

Can you handle multiple jobs in one visit?

Absolutely — this is one of our biggest advantages. Because we cover multiple trades under one roof, we can send one of our team for smaller combined jobs, or coordinate two specialists on the same day. Just describe everything you need in your estimate request and we'll plan it accordingly.
