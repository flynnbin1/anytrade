Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Any Tradesman collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our website or contact us about our services.

1 Who We Are

Any Tradesman is a North Dublin home maintenance and trade services company. We provide electrical, plumbing, carpentry, bathroom renovation and related services to homeowners and businesses across North Dublin.

Contact: office@anytradesman.ie  ·  01 691 7151

2 What Information We Collect

When you submit an estimate request through our website, we collect the following information:

• Your name
• Phone number
• Email address
• Your area or location in North Dublin
• A description of the job or service you require

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

• To respond to your estimate request
• To contact you about your job enquiry
• To improve our services and better understand our customers’ needs

4 Who We Share It With

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by our CRM software for lead management and enquiry tracking purposes. This software operates under its own data protection terms.

5 How Long We Keep It

We retain enquiry data for up to 12 months from the date of submission. After this period, data is deleted unless there is an ongoing relationship or legal requirement to retain it.

6 Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, update or deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time. To exercise these rights, please email us at office@anytradesman.ie and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7 Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly and to store your cookie consent preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are set without your consent. See our cookie notice at the bottom of this page for more details.

8 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch:

Email: office@anytradesman.ie
Phone: 01 691 7151
