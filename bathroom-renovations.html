How It Works

Our renovation process

A bathroom renovation involves more trades than most jobs. We coordinate every step so you don't have to manage multiple contractors.

1

Initial Survey & Design

We visit the property, take measurements and discuss your vision. We advise on layout, materials and realistic costs.

2

Strip Out & Preparation

Old sanitaryware, tiles and floor coverings are removed. Walls and floors are prepared and any damp issues addressed.

3

First Fix Plumbing

All waste and supply pipework is moved or installed before walls and floors are finished.

4

Tiling

Floor and wall tiles are laid to a high standard. We work with tiles you supply or source them for you.

5

Second Fix & Fitting

Sanitaryware, shower, baths, vanity units and accessories are installed and connected.

6

Final Snagging & Clean

We do a final walkthrough, fix any snags, and leave the bathroom spotless and ready to use.
