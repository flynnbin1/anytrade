Our Process

How a bathroom renovation works

1

Free design consultation

We visit your home, take measurements and bring tile samples and sanitaryware brochures. This gives us everything we need to price accurately.

2

Fixed-price written quote

You receive a detailed, written fixed price — no provisional sums, no surprises. All supply and fit included. You decide in your own time.

3

Scheduled start date

We agree a start date, order materials and confirm the programme. Most bathrooms are completed within 5–7 working days from strip-out.

4

Handover & 6-year guarantee

We walk through the finished bathroom with you, snag anything you're not happy with, and issue your 6-year workmanship guarantee in writing.
