Our Process

How we build your media wall

Every media wall is built to your space, your style and your budget. Here's how the process works from start to finish.

1

Design & Planning

We visit, measure and sketch out the design with you — TV position, shelving, alcoves, fireplace integration and any lighting you want included.

2

Frame & Build

Timber stud frame built to your wall dimensions. MDF panels and shelving fitted for a seamless, flush finish throughout.

3

TV Mounting & Cabling

TV bracket installed at the correct height and angle. All cables — HDMI, power, aerial, Ethernet — routed behind the wall for a completely clean look.

4

Finishing & Decoration

Skim coat, prime and paint in your chosen colour. Feature panelling, LED lighting strips or alcove niches added as required. Spotless tidy-up on completion.
